# DocuTrack
**LPU Document Management System** — User Interface

DocuTrack is a web-based application that lets you submit, track, and manage your documents for routing and approval — all from one personal workspace.

---

## Key Features

- View your document statuses at a glance from your personal dashboard
- Submit documents for routing and approval in a few clicks
- Receive notifications when your documents are approved or rejected
- Edit your profile and toggle dark mode from the Preferences page

---

## Getting Started

1. Open `login.html` in any modern web browser
2. Click **Create one** to register a new account
3. Enter your name, email address, and password
4. You will be redirected to your dashboard upon successful registration

---

## User Guide

**Submitting a Document**
1. Select **My Documents** from the sidebar
2. Click **Upload New Document**
3. Enter a document title, choose a type, and click **Submit to Routing**
4. The document will appear in your list with a *Pending Approval* status

**Tracking Your Documents**
- The **Dashboard** displays a live summary of your pending and approved documents
- **My Documents** shows your complete submission history with current status indicators

**Viewing Notifications**
- Open **Notifications** to see a full log of activity related to your documents

**Managing Your Preferences**
- Go to **Preferences** to update your name, contact number, or enable dark mode
- Click **Save Changes** to apply any updates

---

## Important Notes

- All data is stored locally in your browser and is not synced across devices or accounts
- Submitted documents are record entries only — no actual files are uploaded or stored
- Clearing your browser data will permanently remove all records
